Diode

Made of precision machined poly-carbonate, Diode aims to provide you with a simple, fool-proof, sturdy and portable solution to missing keyboard back-lighting or ambient illumination while working on a laptop.

Its plug and go design is user-friendly : you simply plug it to one of your available USB ports and the calibrated LED angles will take care of lighting your laptop keyboard all the way up to 16".

When you're done, simply push it back into its wafer-stamped, copper wire stitched neoprene sleeve, and off you go!

Using LED lights for such an object was decided on their low power consumption and high lighting efficiency: aimed at being a portable workstation's best friend, staying 'on' longer simply makes it better.
