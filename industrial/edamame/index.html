Edamame

Edamame (えだまめ) is the Japanese term or word for twig bean, more commonly known as the soya bean pod.

This concept envisions a smart, natural yet simple approach to feeling good while resting, and bringing a minimal effort into displacing garden furniture according to one's preferences in the surrounding space.

Edamame is light, elegant and provides that breath of fresh air by its green and soothing feel while providing support and balance to the user.
